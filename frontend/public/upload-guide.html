How to Upload Recordings to EchoGuard

This guide will walk you through the process of uploading audio recordings to EchoGuard for compliance analysis.

Step 1: Log in to EchoGuard

Visit the EchoGuard URL provided by your administrator and log in with your credentials.

If you don't have an account yet, click "Create Account" and follow the registration process.

Step 2: Navigate to the Upload Page

Once logged in, click on the "Upload" button in the navigation menu.

Step 3: Select Audio File

Click the "Choose File" button or drag and drop your audio file into the designated area.

Tip: EchoGuard supports MP3, WAV, M4A, and FLAC audio formats. Files should be under 100MB.

Step 4: Add Metadata (Optional)

You can add additional information about your recording:

  • Description: Brief description of the recording content
  • Category: Select a category (e.g., Customer Call, Meeting)
  • Tags: Add relevant tags to help with organization

Step 5: Upload

Click the "Upload" button to start the upload process.

A progress bar will appear showing the upload status.

Important: Do not close the browser window until the upload is complete.

Step 6: Wait for Processing

After the upload completes, EchoGuard will:

  1. Transcribe the audio (typically takes 1-3 minutes)
  2. Analyze the transcript for compliance issues (typically takes 30-60 seconds)
  3. Generate a compliance report

You'll be redirected to the dashboard where you can see the processing status.

Step 7: View Results

Once processing is complete, click on the recording to view:

  • Full transcript of the audio
  • Overall compliance score
  • Specific compliance issues detected
  • Recommendations for improvement
  • Dual AI analysis results

Troubleshooting

Upload fails: Ensure your file is in a supported format and under 100MB.

Processing takes too long: Large files may take longer to process. Check back in 5-10 minutes.

Audio quality issues: For best results, use clear recordings with minimal background noise.
